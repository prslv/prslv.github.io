Update: Introducing YouTube Fast Forward 2.4

YouTube Fast Forward version 2.4 is here!

What's New:

  • Fixed: buttons not displaying on the player in 'Brave' browser, after new UI updates pushed by YouTube.

  • New: 'Report an issue' button added to the bottom left corner of the extension pop-up.

Note: 

Updates can often cause issues. Sometimes it's after a browser update, or simple UI tweaks the developers at YouTube make.

If you are experiencing a problem of any kind after a browser update, please try the following:

  1. Remove the extension;
  2. Clear your browser's cache (images, files, etc.);
  3. Reinstall the extension and test.

* If problems persist, contact me directly by email (kunovpreslav@gmail.com) with more details on exactly what is wrong and when it occured.

The popup works only on YouTube pages.
